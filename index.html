About

Prathmesh P. Vanmali

Founder, Principal Architect

Prathmesh is a young qualified Architect graduated from Kamla Raheja Institute for Architecture & Environmental Studies (KRVIA), Registered with the Council of Architecture (COA), India. He has practiced in Mumbai and has a collective professional experience working with well known architects like Ar. Hafeez contractor, Ar. Snehal Parekh. He also has an international exposure working on Dubai and UAE based projects in collaboration with Er.Khaleel. Prathmesh is a passionate designer who is as interested in the challenges of solving design problems as he is in building relationships with the people who share his passion. He is understanding and extremely sensitive towards client's finer requirements, budget of the project and time constraints.
