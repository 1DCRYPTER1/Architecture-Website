About Us

PV Architecture & Interior Design was established in 2019, Our Principal Architect Mr. Prathmesh Vanmali after his graduation worked with Ar. Hafeez Contractor & Ar. Snehal Parekh and Associates. He also has an international exposure working in Dubai, UAE. Our work focuses on creating relationship between people & the built spaces through new concepts with constant innovation & a continuous process of design appraisal of our own work thus creating more civilized & successful built environment with our architectural design in various sectors from villas, bungalows, residential buildings, urban design, and townships to IT buildings, commercial development, retail & hospitality design. Constant interaction & exchanging ideas with clients help us live it to their expectation & delivering the best & most innovative design solutions in all terms from best design product for the actual user to optimum commercial returns for our clients.
